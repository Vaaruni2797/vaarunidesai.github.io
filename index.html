About Me

I’m an AI researcher focused on interpretability, training dynamics, and transformer-based reasoning. My recent work analyzes how syntactic and reasoning circuits emerge across training using probing pipelines, attention metrics, and representation dynamics. I’ve also built large-scale compute pipelines and designed frameworks such as pickucot to study causal hint use and explanation faithfulness in small language models. Before moving into research, I developed large-scale, high-performance REST APIs at Refinitiv (LSEG) and Thomson Reuters. I hold a Master’s in Data Science & Engineering from UC San Diego.
